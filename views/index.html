The Soul of the Brand

We believe that your “best life” isn’t a destination you reach — it’s a collection of intentional choices you make every single morning.

It’s the weight of a linen sheet, the aroma of a morning espresso in a ceramic cup, the financial freedom to say “yes” to adventure, and the holistic wellness that keeps you vibrant enough to enjoy it all.

We are Kaz and Meli, the mother-daughter duo behind ELATEVE. Born and raised in the heart of London, we’ve spent the last 20 years immersed in the soul of Continental Europe. From the sun-drenched terraces of Barcelona to the chic ateliers of Paris, we haven’t just visited these places — we’ve built lives, businesses, and homes within them.

✦

The Minds Behind the Curation

Meli The Eye for Luxury & Lifestyle

With 10 years in high-end real estate and luxury markets across Barcelona, Paris and London, Meli has spent her career inside some of the world’s most beautiful homes. She understands the “je ne sais quoi” of European aesthetics — that effortless blend of modern function and timeless elegance. Her mission is to bring that elevated lifestyle to your doorstep.

Meli knows that a beautiful home or a chic wardrobe is only as good as the woman living inside it. She understands the weight of modern expectations — the burnout that comes when self-care falls to the bottom of the to-do list. Through her own journey, she discovered that elevating your life starts between the ears. By shifting her mindset from “surviving the day” to “curating the moment,” she unlocked a level of presence and confidence that changed everything.

She is a firm believer that how you show up for yourself — through movement, mental clarity, and radical kindness — dictates how the world shows up for you.

✦

The Soul of the Brand — Sabrina’s Story

Two years ago, in the sun-scorched streets of Granada, Southern Spain, Meli found a soul in need: a starving, abandoned rescue dog. Sabrina was a shadow of herself, but even in her weakest state, she possessed a quiet resilience.

Meli set out to rescue Sabrina — but as any dog lover knows, the roles quickly reversed. Sabrina rescued Meli. Watching Sabrina transform from a frightened stray into a joyful, thriving companion taught Meli the ultimate lesson in the ELATEVE philosophy:

Recovery is possible with the right environment.
Luxury is a feeling of safety and being cared for.
Resilience is beautiful, and even the most “broken” things can be restored to a state of grace.

Today, Sabrina is a constant fixture in the ELATEVE world — reminding us daily that wellness isn’t just for humans, and that a life lived to the full always includes a wagging tail and a grateful heart.

✦
Kaz The Architect of Home & Heart

An entrepreneur for over 35 years, Kaz has mastered the art of the pivot and the polish. Having raised a family of three while renovating historic properties from crumbling wrecks into sanctuaries, she knows that a home must be as functional as it is beautiful. Her business expertise spans the service and investment industries, where she has been recognised as a highly effective leader and speaker. Her passion is connecting people with the knowledge to make a beautiful life, accessible to all.

Kaz is living proof that your middle years aren’t a plateau — they are a launchpad. For her, curiosity isn’t just a trait; it’s a survival skill. She believes that the moment we stop learning is the moment we stop living.

There was a time when the fog of midlife felt heavy. Instead of accepting it as inevitable, Kaz looked to her surroundings in Barcelona. By embracing the Mediterranean lifestyle — seasonal whole foods, daily movement, and the radical act of slowing down — she didn’t just find her clarity again; she amplified it. This led to a complete culinary evolution: starting with very little kitchen confidence, she immersed herself in the local markets of Spain and today blends the heart-healthy fats of the Mediterranean with the anti-inflammatory spices of Asian cuisine.

Kaz’s wellness philosophy is built on a 360° approach — mind and body as an inseparable duo:

Weightlifting

At 56, Kaz picked up a barbell for the first time. She advocates for strength training for women — for bone density, posture, and the confidence that comes from feeling physically capable.

Sustainable Vitality

Long walks along the Balearic coast and hiking the Montserrat trails — heart health without the burnout.

The Nourishment Ritual

From the deliberate preparation of a matcha tea to a cold-water dip or a forest path — rituals that replenish the spirit.

“I spent decades building businesses and a family. Now, I’m building myself. My goal is to show women that you can be stronger, sharper, and more vibrant at 60 than you were at 30.”
