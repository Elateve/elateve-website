We believe that longevity isn’t about chasing more years — it’s about feeling clear, capable, and like yourself at every one of them.

We are Kaz and Meli, the mother-daughter duo behind ELATEVE. Between us, we’ve navigated postpartum recovery, entrepreneurial burnout, and perimenopause — and the quiet confusion of not knowing which protocol, product, or professional to trust. We built ELATEVE because we were tired of guessing.

The Women Behind ELATEVE

Meli The Longevity Curator

After a decade spent inside the luxury and lifestyle world across Barcelona, Paris and London, Meli learned early that beauty without vitality is just decoration. Her own burnout in her late twenties — the exhaustion, the hormonal chaos, the doctors with no real answers — became her wake-up call.

She started digging into the longevity science herself: testing protocols, sitting through consultations, and asking every specialist and clinic she could find the questions other women were too busy or too embarrassed to ask. Through that journey, she discovered that elevating your life starts between the ears — and in your bloodwork. By shifting her focus from “surviving the day” to understanding what her body actually needed at each stage, she unlocked a level of clarity and energy that changed everything.

She is a firm believer that real transformation starts with the right information, from the right professionals, tailored to exactly where you are.

✦
Kaz The Midlife Trailblazer

An entrepreneur for over 35 years, Kaz has mastered the art of the pivot. But nothing prepared her for perimenopause — the brain fog, the sleepless nights, and being handed a leaflet and told to push through it. She refused to accept that fading quietly was the only option.

Kaz is living proof that your middle years aren’t a plateau — they are a launchpad. For her, curiosity isn’t just a trait; it’s a survival skill. She believes that the moment we stop learning about our own bodies is the moment we stop living fully.

There was a time when the fog of midlife felt heavy. Instead of accepting it as inevitable, Kaz sought out the specialists, the bloodwork, and the movement science that actually explained what was happening to her. She didn’t just find her clarity again — she built a new relationship with her own biology, one most women are never taught.

Kaz’s wellness philosophy is built on a 360° approach — mind and body as an inseparable duo:

Weightlifting

At 56, Kaz picked up a barbell for the first time. She advocates for strength training for women — for bone density, hormone health, and the confidence that comes from feeling physically capable.

Sustainable Vitality

Long walks along the Balearic coast and hiking the Montserrat trails — heart health without the burnout.

The Nourishment Ritual

From the deliberate preparation of a matcha tea to a cold-water dip or a forest path — rituals that replenish the spirit.

“I spent decades building businesses and a family. Now, I’m building myself. My goal is to show women that you can be stronger, sharper, and more vibrant at 60 than you were at 30.”

Not Just Us — A Team for Every Decade

Longevity looks different at 32 than it does at 58. That’s why ELATEVE isn’t only Kaz and Meli — behind every recommendation is a small team of women in their 30s, 40s, 50s and 60s, each living through her own hormonal chapter: fertility and postpartum recovery, perimenopause, menopause, and the decades beyond. We compare notes, we disagree about what actually works, and we test everything on ourselves before it ever reaches you.

We only work with high-end professionals and medical-grade equipment — no guesswork, no gimmicks, no wellness trend dressed up as science. If we wouldn’t put it in our own bodies or recommend it to our own mothers, sisters, and daughters, it doesn’t make the cut.

Because the supplement aisle and the longevity world can feel overwhelming, and frankly a little scary — and no woman should have to navigate it alone.
